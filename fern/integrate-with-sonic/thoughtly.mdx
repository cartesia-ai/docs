Thoughtly is an easy-to-use platform to build fully-integrated voice agents for your business. Build workflows around your CRM and let your agents handle the rest. 

Cartesia voices and cloning capabilities are available on [Thoughtly's platform](https://docs.thoughtly.com/platform/voices) today - find a Cartesia voice in their voice library or bring your own using Thoughtly's cloning capabilities, exclusively powered by Cartesia.

## Who we are

At Cartesia we are very excited about using AI to amplify human expression and creativity. Our vision is to reimagine multimedia and provide the tools to conceive highly personalizable multi-modal content at real-time speeds. We build off years of deep learning expertise through research pioneered by our team to deliver AI models that solve challenging problems at blazing speeds.&#x20;

## What we offer

{% hint style="info" %}
We highly recommend trying out our models on the playground at [play.cartesia.ai](https://play.cartesia.ai)!
{% endhint %}

Our current focus is on expanding the frontier of what we can do in the audio and conversational space. To that effect, we are very excited to launch our **Audio API** which provides the following features:

### Text-to-speech model

We provide a state-of-the-art **text-to-speech generation** model with **real-time** and top-of-class latency. Our flagship model can generate 1s of audio with as low as 0.65s processing time and 150ms latency - it ranks amongst the lowest on the market!

{% hint style="info" %}
Given a transcript and a predefined voice, a text-to-speech model generates an audio clip of the voice uttering the given transcript.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="info" %}
We currently only support English but are adding support for many more languages and plan to release a beta sometime soon !&#x20;
{% endhint %}

### Library of voices

We share a growing **library of voices** with different genders, ages, accents, personalities, ...&#x20;

They have been categorized according to their target audience: gaming, call center, narration, ASMR.

{% hint style="info" %}
The current library is only the beginning, we'll be adding more voices in the future. If you have any special requests please reach out to us!
{% endhint %}

### Voice mixing

We offer the ability to **mix different voices** together, and personalize each voice by adding filters that amplify specific voice characteristics.

{% hint style="info" %}
We are working hard to add more control and better fidelity. We will post more about this in future updates.
{% endhint %}

### Instant voice cloning

We are very excited to share our **instant voice cloning** feature which enables our text-to-speech model to imitate a voice captured in any given audio clip. This feature is only available as a paid functionality to help prevent misuse. To try out voice cloning, head to the playground at [play.cartesia.ai](https://play.cartesia.ai) and click on "Clone a voice".&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
We are aware of the potential misuse that can come out of this feature, in particular the ability to generate a deepfake and impersonate someone. Please be mindful to only upload the voices of the people for which you have explicit consent. We will closely monitor the usage of this feature and implement safeguards to limit its misuse.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
